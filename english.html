Emil Strumiński

I have been a personal coach for 5 years. What is more, I have a university MA title in physiotherapy, I am a physical education an corrective gymnastics teacher. I have a postgraduate diploma in Nutrition and Supplementing in Sports. I am bodybuilding and swimmning instructor, an international European organisation EFA trainer. I participate in the following courses: B.E.S.T. Training, Functional motor assessment (Centrum Synergia), Fitness Trainer Licence B, Functional Training(EFS), Dieting and Supplementing (Mauricz).

In my opinion the key idea of personal training is the process of monitoring the client’s achievements and reacting to their body response by adjusting the diet and physical effort. That is why, each client is subjected to a unique idea and training programme. The variety of exercises, correctly adjusted intensity and the commitment of the trainee allows to maximize the effects in the shortest possible period.

I have been involved in sport since my early childhood. As a child I received numerous gold medals in swimming at the Silesia region level. When I was 14 I started my 10-year adventure with handball. I played for the premier-league team ‘CMC Viret Zawiercie’. Now I am fully committed to my work with my clients. In my free time a do amateur training mainly experimenting with various training methods in endurance and strength sports.
